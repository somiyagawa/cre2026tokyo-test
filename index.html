Welcome to CRE 2026 Tokyo

We are delighted to invite you to the 27th International Conference on Current Research in Egyptology, taking place in the vibrant city of Tokyo from July 11-15, 2026.

International Gathering

Join scholars from around the world in discussing the latest research in Egyptology, covering periods from Prehistoric times through the Islamic era. This conference brings together leading experts and emerging researchers in a collaborative atmosphere.

Academic Excellence

Hosted by the University of Tsukuba Tokyo Campus, the conference provides a platform for presenting cutting-edge research, fostering academic dialogue, and building international collaborations in the field of Egyptology.

Important Dates

Early November 2025

Call for Papers Opens

Abstract submission begins. Scholars are invited to submit their research proposals covering any aspect of Egyptian civilization from Prehistoric to Islamic periods.

January 16, 2026

Abstract Submission Deadline

Final deadline for abstract submissions. Extensions of 1-2 weeks may be granted based on submission volume and peer review capacity.

Late February 2026

Notification & First Circular

Authors will be notified of acceptance decisions. The First Circular with detailed conference information will be distributed to all participants.

May 2026

Second Circular

Final program details, registration information, and logistical updates will be shared with all registered participants.

July 11-15, 2026

CRE 2026 Conference

Five days of presentations, panel discussions, keynote lectures, and networking opportunities in the heart of Tokyo. Join us for this memorable academic gathering.

August 2026

Proceedings Call for Papers

Authors will be invited to submit full papers for the conference proceedings, ensuring the dissemination of research presented at CRE 2026 Tokyo.

Conference Highlights

Keynote Lectures

Distinguished scholars will deliver inspiring keynote presentations on groundbreaking research in Egyptology, setting the tone for rich academic discourse.

Panel Discussions

Engage in thematic panel sessions covering diverse aspects of Egyptian studies, from archaeology and philology to digital humanities and conservation.

Networking Events

Connect with fellow researchers, establish collaborations, and build lasting professional relationships during dedicated networking sessions and social events.

Conference Proceedings

Selected papers will be published in the conference proceedings, providing a permanent record of the latest research presented at CRE 2026 Tokyo.

Support for Egyptian Scholars

We are committed to supporting Egyptian researchers through our crowdfunding initiative, ensuring diverse international participation.

Cultural Experience

Experience the unique blend of tradition and modernity in Tokyo, with opportunities to explore Japanese culture alongside your academic pursuits.

Join Us in Tokyo

Be part of this prestigious gathering of Egyptologists in one of the world's most fascinating cities. Submit your research, register for the conference, and prepare for an enriching academic experience.
