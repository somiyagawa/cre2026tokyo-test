Our Mission

CRE 2026 Tokyo is committed to fostering truly international dialogue in Egyptology. We believe that Egyptian scholars—whose heritage we study—must have a strong voice at this conference. To make this possible, we are launching a crowdfunding campaign to support travel and accommodation for Egyptian researchers.

Why Support Egyptian Scholars?

Diverse Perspectives

Egyptian scholars bring unique insights and perspectives rooted in their cultural heritage and direct connection to the civilization we study.

Equitable Access

International travel costs can be prohibitive. Our support program ensures financial barriers don't prevent qualified researchers from participating.

Global Collaboration

By supporting Egyptian participation, we strengthen international networks and foster meaningful collaborations that benefit the entire field.

Crowdfunding Campaign

Powered by Readyfor Inc.

Crowdfunding Details Coming Soon

Our crowdfunding campaign, managed through Readyfor Inc., will launch soon. The campaign will detail our funding goals, how contributions will be used, and the rewards available for supporters.

Campaign Focus
  • Round-trip airfare for Egyptian scholars
  • Accommodation during the conference
  • Registration fee waivers
  • Per diem support for meals and local transportation

How You Can Help

Make a Contribution

Once our crowdfunding campaign launches, you'll be able to make a direct contribution to support Egyptian scholars. Every donation, no matter the size, makes a difference.

Spread the Word

Share our campaign with your colleagues, institutions, and networks. Help us reach a wider audience and maximize our impact.

Institutional Support

If you represent an institution, consider making a corporate or institutional contribution. We offer recognition opportunities for major sponsors.

Volunteer

Help us promote the campaign, organize fundraising events, or assist with conference logistics to support our Egyptian participants.

Transparency & Accountability

We are committed to complete transparency in how funds are used. All contributions will be managed through Readyfor Inc., a trusted crowdfunding platform. We will provide regular updates on:

  • Total funds raised
  • Number of scholars supported
  • Breakdown of expenses
  • Impact reports after the conference
