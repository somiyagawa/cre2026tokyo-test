University of Tsukuba Tokyo Campus

The conference will be held at the University of Tsukuba Tokyo Campus, located in the heart of Tokyo with excellent access to public transportation.

Address

3-29-1 Otsuka, Bunkyo-ku
Tokyo 112-0012, Japan

Nearest Station

Myogadani Station
Tokyo Metro Marunouchi Line
(5-minute walk)

Access from Major Stations

  • Tokyo Station
    15 minutes via Marunouchi Line
  • Shinjuku Station
    20 minutes via Marunouchi Line
  • Haneda Airport
    50 minutes via Tokyo Monorail + Marunouchi Line
  • Narita Airport
    90 minutes via Narita Express + Marunouchi Line
