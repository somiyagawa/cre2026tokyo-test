Call for Papers
دعوة لتقديم الأوراق البحثية

We invite scholars to submit abstracts for presentation at CRE 2026 Tokyo
ندعو الباحثين لتقديم ملخصات الأبحاث للعرض في مؤتمر طوكيو ٢٠٢٦

Deadline: January 16, 2026
الموعد النهائي: ١٦ يناير ٢٠٢٦

The CRE Japan Committee cordially invites researchers, scholars, and students to submit abstracts for the 27th International Conference on Current Research in Egyptology, to be held from July 11-15, 2026, at the University of Tsukuba Tokyo Campus.

This conference provides a platform for presenting the latest research covering all aspects of Egyptian civilization from Prehistoric to Islamic periods, including but not limited to archaeology, philology, art history, religion, digital humanities, and cultural heritage.

Important Deadline

Abstract Submission Deadline: January 16, 2026
Extensions of 1-2 weeks may be granted based on submission volume.

Research Topics

We welcome submissions on all aspects of Egyptian civilization from Prehistoric to Islamic periods

Archaeology & Material Culture

  • Excavation reports and site studies
  • Pottery, stone tools, and artifacts
  • Settlement patterns and urbanism
  • Funerary archaeology and burial practices

Philology & Linguistics

  • Hieroglyphic, Hieratic, and Demotic texts
  • Coptic language and literature
  • Papyrology and ostraca studies
  • Computational linguistics

Art & Architecture

  • Temple and tomb architecture
  • Sculpture and relief decoration
  • Painting and decorative arts
  • Iconography and symbolism

Religion & Society

  • Religious texts and beliefs
  • Ritual practices and cult
  • Social history and daily life
  • Gender studies and identity

Digital Humanities

  • Digital epigraphy and text analysis
  • 3D modeling and visualization
  • Database development
  • Computational analysis methods

Cultural Heritage

  • Conservation and preservation
  • Museum studies and curation
  • Heritage management
  • Public archaeology and outreach

Submission Guidelines

Abstract Requirements

  • Length: 300-500 words (excluding title and references)
  • Language: English
  • Format: Plain text or PDF via Google Form
  • Content: Clear statement of research question, methodology, and key findings
  • Author Information: Full name, institutional affiliation, email address, and nationality

Presentation Formats

Accepted presentations will be scheduled in one of the following formats:

  • Individual Papers: 20-minute presentation + 10 minutes Q&A
  • Panel Sessions: Thematic sessions with 3-4 speakers (90 minutes total)
  • Poster Presentations: Visual display with 5-minute lightning talk

Review Process

All abstracts will undergo peer review by the Scientific Committee. Evaluation criteria include:

  • Originality and significance of research
  • Clarity of research question and methodology
  • Relevance to Egyptological studies
  • Quality of abstract writing

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit multiple abstracts?

Yes, scholars may submit more than one abstract, but each submission will be evaluated independently. You may present a maximum of two papers at the conference.

Are graduate students eligible to submit?

Absolutely! We encourage graduate students at all levels (MA and PhD) to submit their research. This is an excellent opportunity to share your work with the international Egyptological community.

What if my research is still in progress?

Ongoing research is welcome. Your abstract should clearly state your research question, methodology, and preliminary findings or expected outcomes.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, accepted presenters will need to register for the conference. Registration fees and details will be provided in the acceptance notification. Please see our Registration page for more information.

Will proceedings be published?

Yes! Selected papers will be invited for publication in the conference proceedings. A separate call for full papers will be issued in August 2026 following the conference.

Can I request an extension for the submission deadline?

Extensions of 1-2 weeks may be granted based on submission volume and the capacity of the Scientific Committee for peer review. Please contact us as early as possible if you need an extension.
